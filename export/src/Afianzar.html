04 · Where it breaks

Seven stages.
Three moments of truth.

We followed María through the full onboarding journey. The story isn't what she does — it's where she gets stuck.
What is failing in the experience

Three symptoms.
One broken experience.

At every moment of truth, the system fails María — and she absorbs the cost in front of the client.
Symptom 01 · Stage 04 Submission

Fragmented submission.

Documents trickle in across WhatsApp, email and phone — partial, out of order, sometimes lost in the thread. The agency never knows if the package is complete.

María says
"I feel invisible. Powerless. Completely out of control."
~30% of submissions arrive incomplete
→ study can't begin.
Symptom 02 · Stage 05 Review

Invisible process.

Once submitted, the case enters a black box. No tracking, no ETA, no acknowledgment. The agency has nothing to tell the client — just silence.

María says
"I feel like a middleman with no clue what's happening."
4–24 hours of silence
→ clients consider other properties.
Symptom 03 · Stage 06 Decision

Endless relay loops.

Any missing document triggers a four-actor relay. María chases the tenant, the tenant chases the bank, and every round adds days. Each loop feels like starting from zero.

María says
"Each loop feels like starting from zero. I'm exhausted."
+1 to +3 days per loop
→ deals quietly die.
"The agency's reputation is directly affected by Afianzar's performance — they are the face of the service."
Why it matters
05 · The intervention

Three nudges.
One quiet rewiring.

We don't replace the process. We remove the friction at the points where it bends — one moment of truth at a time.
Where we intervene — and how

Four moments. Four targeted nudges.

Each nudge reduces friction, builds trust, and moves the deal forward — without re-engineering the business.
Stage 04 · Submission
Submit once,
complete.
The problem
30% of submissions arrive incomplete. Each missing doc triggers a relay loop adding 1–3 days.
The nudge
A profile-specific digital checklist. María submits one structured form — no email, no fragments.
Incomplete rate < 5%
Stage 05 · Acknowledgment
End the
silence.
The problem
4–24 hours of silence after submission. María has nothing to report. Tenants start considering other properties.
The nudge
Automatic confirmation the moment the case is received and opened — within one hour.
Acknowledgment < 1 hour
Stage 05 · Review
Automate the
scoring.
The problem
Operator manually uploads docs to multiple consulting platforms to assess credit score and risk — the bottleneck.
The nudge
API rules consult the platforms automatically and return a verdict: approve, manual review, or reject. Humans only handle edge cases.
Auto-decision rate > 70%
Stage 06 · Decision
Break the
relay loop.
The problem
Any missing doc triggers a 4-actor chain adding 1–3 days. María deflects to Afianzar instead of managing the tenant.
The nudge
Afianzar contacts the tenant directly for missing docs. María gets a notification. Loop eliminated.
Correction loop < 10%
